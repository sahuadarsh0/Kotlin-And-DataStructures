#### `Easy` `CodeChef` Alternative Square Pattern

```
You're given a number N. Print the first N lines of the below-given pattern.

1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
11 12 13 14 15
20 19 18 17 16
21 22 23 24 25
30 29 28 27 26
Input:
First-line will contain the number N.
Output:
Print the first N lines of the given pattern.

Constraints
1≤N≤200
Sample Input 1:
4
Sample Output 1:
1 2 3 4 5
10 9 8 7 6
11 12 13 14 15
20 19 18 17 16
```
